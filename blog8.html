Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrival in Danang ( Lunch/ Dinner)

Upon arrival at Da Nang Airport, you will be greeted by our tour guide and driver. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before starting your city tour. Visit Bien Dong Park, Love Lock Bridge, Dragon Bridge, and My Khe Beach to catch a beautiful sunset. After dinner, explore and shop at Da Nang Night Market. Overnight in Da Nang.

  • Day 2: Da Nang – Son Tra Peninsula - Cam Thanh Coconut Jungle - Hoian Ancient Town (Breakfast | Lunch | Dinner)

Start your day with breakfast at the hotel before heading to Son Tra Peninsula, where breathtaking views of Da Nang await from the mountaintop. Continue to the Fishing Village and Cam Thanh Coconut Forest for a glimpse into local life. After lunch, enjoy a memorable basket boat ride through the coconut groves (optional $5) and experience the lively basket boat dance. In the evening, visit Hoi An Ancient Town to see the Japanese Bridge, Phuc Kien Pagoda, and vibrant lantern-lit streets. Shop for souvenirs and have dinner at a local restaurant. Return to Da Nang for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3: Da Nang – Ba Na Hills - Golden Hand Bridge (Breakfast | Lunch | Dinner)

After breakfast, head to Ba Na Hills, 30 km from Da Nang. Take a cable car to the top, walk along the iconic Golden Hand Bridge, and explore the French Village, flower gardens, and Fantasy Park. Enjoy an international buffet lunch, then descend by cable car and shop at Han Market. End the day with dinner at a local restaurant and an overnight stay in Da Nang.

  • Day 4: Danang Departure (Breakfast)

After breakfast, check out by 12:00 PM. Enjoy free time at your leisure until your transfer to the airport for your flight home. Thank you for choosing our tour! We hope you had an amazing experience and look forward to welcoming you again!
