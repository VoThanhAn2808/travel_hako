Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Phu Quoc Arrival ( Dinner)

After check-in, explore the island starting with Khu Tuong pepper farm and Ham Ninh fishing village to experience local culture and traditional fishing techniques. Visit the historic Phu Quoc Prison, then head to An Thoi local market to discover the local specialties and maybe try some delicious seafood. Overnight in Phu Quoc.

  • Day 2: Four Island Tour in Phu Quoc(Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

After breakfast, head to An Thoi port for your four-island tour, with snorkeling gear provided to explore coral reefs and marine life. Visit Pineapple, Fingernail, Dam Ngang, and May Rut Islands to enjoy stunning beaches and clear waters. After lunch, relax at Sao Beach, one of Phu Quoc's most beautiful spots, and visit a pearl shop to learn about the island's pearl farming. In the evening, enjoy dinner and explore Phu Quoc's vibrant night market for souvenirs and street food. Overnight in Phu Quoc.

  • Day 3: Free and Easy Day in Phu Quoc ( Breakfast)

Breakfast at the hotel. Spend full day free and easy on the beautiful beach or discover Phu Quoc island by yourselves. Overnight in Phu Quoc.

  • Day 4: Phu Quoc Departure ( Breakfast)

Have breakfast at the hotel and check out before 12.00 PM. Free and easy at your leisure until transfer to the airport for your departing flight home sweet home. End of our services.
