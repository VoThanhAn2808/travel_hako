Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive – Phnom Penh, Sour Sdey “Hello” Cambodia

Upon arrival at Phnom Penh Bus Station, meet with our driver and guide for a direct transfer to visit the Royal Palace compound, built in 1866 by the predecessors of King Norodom. This is one of the most prominent and impressive colorful Khmer-style palaces. Next to the Royal Palace is the Silver Pagoda (the Emerald Buddha temple), displaying numerous Buddha statues decorated and crafted from diamonds, emeralds, gold, and silver. Continue to the National Museum, a distinctive red building with beautiful Khmer architecture, built in 1917, containing more than 5,000 art objects made of sandstone, bronze, and silver. Overnight in Phnom Penh.

  • Day 2: Phnom Penh – Siem Reap ( Breakfast)

After breakfast, departure from Phnom Penh to Siem Reap. Upon arrival, transfer to the hotel for check-in and the rest of the evening is free at your own time. Overnight in Siem Reap.

  • Day 3: Angkor Highlight ( Breakfast)

Start the day with breakfast at the hotel. Drive to Angkor Thom, King Jayavarman VII’s masterpiece fortress built after Angkor’s occupation by the Chams. Visit Bayon Temple at the center, a unique reflection of the king’s vision. Stop at the Terrace of the Leper King and stroll along the Terrace of Elephants, originally for royal ceremonies, with Baphuon Temple at the southern end. Explore the enchanting Ta Prohm, where tree roots intertwine with the temple ruins, creating a hauntingly beautiful scene. Finally, visit Angkor Wat, the world’s largest religious monument and a source of pride for the Khmer people. Overnight in Siem Reap.

  • Day 4: Siem Reap – Departure ( Breakfast)

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Free until transfer to Siem Reap airport for departure to next destination. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
