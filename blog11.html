Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrival in Danang

Upon arrival at Danang International Airport, you will be greeted by our representative. They will assist you with the transfer to the hotel for check-in (subject to room availability, check-in time is after 2:00 PM). Afterward, you will be taken to a local restaurant for lunch. In the evening, enjoy the stunning atmosphere in Da Nang City.

  • Day 02: Danang - BRG Golf Club - Danang (Breakfast)

Start your day with breakfast at the hotel before being picked up and transferred to BRG Golf Club for a round of golf. Spend your time enjoying the championship Dunes course, designed by Greg Norman and Harley Kruse, which stretches 7,160 yards and is known for its stunning coastal views near My Khe Beach The course features notable holes, including back-to-back par fives at the 4th and 5th and the signature 16th hole, a 148-yard elevated green framed by the East Sea and Cham Islands. After a fulfilling day on the links, you will be transferred back to the hotel for some relaxation. Overnight stay in Danang.

  • Day 03: Danang - Bana Hills Golf Club - Danang (Breakfast)

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel before being picked up and transferred to Ba Na Hills Golf Club for a round of golf. This unique course spans over 7,800 yards and offers a scenic layout with indigenous vegetation. One of its highlights is the ability to play at night under floodlights, featuring 108 poles and 623 halogen lamps. The front nine consists of longer, flatter holes, while the back nine offers elevation changes, including the signature par three 16th hole, which features a dramatic downhill shot to an island green. After your game, return to the hotel for relaxation.

  • Day 04: Danang - Laguna Golf Club Lang Co Hue - Danang (Breakfast)

Start your day with a delicious breakfast at the hotel before being picked up for your transfer to Laguna Golf Club Lang Co, a premier golfing destination designed by six-time Major winner Sir Nick Faldo. Opened in March 2013, this stunning course is located on the East Sea coastline, nestled between the historic cities of Hue and Danang. With its picturesque setting of sandy dunes, mountains, and ocean views, it offers a unique and challenging golfing experience that attracts enthusiasts from around the world. Spend your time enjoying the meticulously crafted layout that showcases the natural beauty of the area. The course is known for its bold design and distinct character, providing golfers with an exciting round amid breathtaking scenery. After your game, you’ll return to the hotel to relax and unwind.

  • Day 05: Departure from Danang (Breakfast)

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast, enjoy some free time before we take you to the airport for your return flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
