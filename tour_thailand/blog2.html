Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Bangkok Arrival

Upon arrival in Bangkok, we will head to Wat Arun. After visiting Wat Arun, you will return to the hotel for check-in and settle in. Enjoy the evening at your leisure, exploring the vibrant city or relaxing. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

  • Day 2: Bangkok- Ayutthaya (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner).

After breakfast, you will head to Ayutthaya and visit Wat Yai Chai Mongkol, which was built in 1351 by King U-Thong and is still an active temple that serves as a residence for monks. You will also visit Wat Phra Si Sanphet. After that, the journey will continue to Wihan Phra Mongkhon Bophit. Finally, you will return to Bangkok for shopping at MBK Centre Shopping Mall.

  • Day 3: Bangkok - Pattaya (B/L/-)

After breakfast, you will head to Pattaya to visit the Honey Shop, where you can learn about Thailand's unique honey. Next, you will explore Frost Magical Ice of Siam, the largest ice sculpture complex in the ASEAN region, and Great & Grand Sweet Destination, a themed park filled with sweet snacks and desserts, reminiscent of Willy Wonka's fantasy world. The next stop is Erawadee Herb Shop, where you can shop for Thailand's beneficial herbal products.

  • Day 4:Pattaya - Bangkok (B)

After breakfast, you will visit the Gems Factory to see beautiful gemstones. Then, you will head to Nongnooch Village to watch the Traditional & Elephant Show. Next, you will visit the Laser Buddha Hill, where you can see a Buddha statue carved with lasers on the hillside. After that, you will be transferred back to Bangkok. On the way, you will stop at the Dried Food & Snack Shop, where you can buy a variety of Thai snacks and souvenirs. Finally, you will visit the Jodd Fair Night Market, where you can try traditional Thai snacks and shop for unique items.

  • Day 5: Bangkok (B/L)

Shopping Day! Today, you will be taken to Platinum Fashion Mall and the Pratunam Shopping Area for some shopping. After that, you will visit Siam Center and Icon Siam.
