Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: HANOI ARRIVE -CITY TOUR

Arrive at Hanoi airport and be greeted by our driver. Transfer to the hotel for luggage drop-off. Next, visit the Ho Chi Minh Complex, including Ba Dinh Square, Ho Chi Minh’s Mausoleum, his stilt house, and the One Pillar Pagoda. Enjoy lunch, then explore the Hoa Lo Prison Museum, known as the “Hanoi Hilton” during the Second Indochina War. Enjoy free time in the evening to relax or explore before your overnight stay in Hanoi.

Lunch is included
  • Day 02 : HANOI – LAO CAI PROVINCE- SAPA- CAT CAT VILLAGE

Depart from your hotel in Hanoi’s Old Quarter and travel by bus to Lao Cai Province, with rest stops en route. Upon arrival in Sapa Town, immerse yourself in the vibrant traditional attire of the H’mong, Dzao, and Tay ethnic groups, followed by a delicious local Vietnamese lunch. Visit Cat Cat Village, the home of the Black H’mong, where you can witness their daily life, trek through scenic landscapes to the waterfall and historic Hydraulic Power Station, and capture memorable photos. Return to Sapa Town for an overnight hotel stay, with free time to enjoy evening activities.

Breakfast and Lunch are included
  • Day 03: SAPA – FANSIPAN LEGENT

After breakfast and hotel check-out, head to the Cable Car Station for your ride to Fansipan Peak. Enjoy a scenic 15-minute cable car journey with stunning views of lush forests and terraced rice fields. Explore the peaceful Ancient Rose Garden and the "Live Museum," showcasing the culture of five ethnic minority groups. Trek the final 600 steps to the summit of Fansipan, known as the "Roof of Indochina," and visit Bich Van Zen Monastery, Thanh Van Dac Lo, and Bao An Zen Monastery. Enjoy a buffet lunch on Fansipan before returning to your hotel in Sapa.

Breakfast and lunch are included
  • Day 04: SAPA –CITY TOUR- BACK TO HANOI

After breakfast at the hotel, start your day with a short walk to Moana View, located just 700m from the church. This trendy spot is perfect for capturing memorable photos at attractions like the Bali Gate, Infinity Lake, Moana Girl statue, Golden Hand, thrilling swing, and the panoramic café with views of Fansipan Peak and Muong Hoa Valley. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before a limousine picks you up from the hotel at 13:30 for your transfer back to Hanoi. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!

Breakfast and lunch are included
