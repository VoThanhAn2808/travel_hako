Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Doha

Today, you are expected to gather at Soekarno Hatta International Airport to begin your European tour, with the first destination being Zurich via Doha.

  • Day 02: Doha – Zurich – St. Gallen – Vaduz – Zurich (Dinner)

Upon arrival at Zurich International Airport, you will be taken to the city of St. Gallen to visit the Baroque-style Cathedral of St. Gallen, and stroll around the bustling Markgasse, where you’ll find paintings and carvings from the 16th and 17th centuries. In the afternoon, you’ll continue your journey to Liechtenstein, one of the smallest countries in the world. There, you will visit the capital, Vaduz, to explore St. Florin Cathedral, Vaduz Castle, and the government building. Later, you’ll head to Zurich for an overnight stay.

  • Day 03: Zurich – Lucerne – Interlaken/Nearby (Breakfast, Dinner)

Today’s tour will take you to Lucerne, where you’ll visit the Lion Monument, Old City, Chapel Bridge, and have time to shop for famous Swiss watches. Then, you’ll travel to Interlaken, a beautiful city nestled between two lakes, Brienzersee and Thunersee, on the Lucerne-Interlaken Express. During the journey, you’ll enjoy Switzerland’s natural beauty. Overnight in Interlaken or nearby.

  • Day 04: Interlaken/Nearby – Jungfraujoch – Interlaken/Nearby (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast, you’ll head to Jungfraujoch, one of the highest mountain peaks in Switzerland, standing at 4,158 meters above sea level. Here, you will visit the Ice Grotto and the glacier with its eternal snow. Afterward, return to Interlaken/Grindelwald for rest.

  • Day 05: Interlaken/Nearby – Zermatt – Tasch/Sion (Breakfast, Dinner)

After breakfast, you’ll continue to Zermatt, a unique and beautiful pollution-free town at the foot of Mt. Matterhorn. Here, you can take an optional tour to the Gornergrat Mountain via the Gornergrat Rack Rail, where you’ll enjoy views of Mt. Matterhorn, the symbol of Toblerone chocolate.

  • Day 06: Tasch/Sion – Montreux – Lausanne (Breakfast, Dinner)

After breakfast, you will continue to Montreux, a city famous for its music festival. Upon arrival, you will pass by Chillon Castle, a European royal heritage, and take photos by Lake Geneva and the Freddy Mercury Monument. Overnight in Lausanne.

  • Day 07: Lausanne – Davos – Lugano (Breakfast, Dinner)

After breakfast, you will head to Davos, where you can experience the Historical Train, which has been in operation since 1920. Enjoy the beautiful views of the Davos area. In the afternoon, the journey continues to Lugano for an overnight stay.

  • Day 08: Lugano – Como – Malpensa (Breakfast, Dinner)

After breakfast, you will travel to Como, where you will explore the famous Lake Como, renowned for its stunning beauty. In the afternoon, you will continue to Malpensa for an overnight stay.

  • Day 09: Malpensa – Doha (Breakfast)

After breakfast, you will visit Milan, Italy’s largest cosmopolitan city, for a photo stop at the Gothic-style Duomo Cathedral. You will also visit Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, where you can enjoy free time for shopping or relaxing at a café. Later, you will be escorted to the airport for your journey back home.

  • Day 10: Doha – Jakarta

Arrive back in Jakarta. Thank you for your participation, and we look forward to seeing you in our future tour programs.
