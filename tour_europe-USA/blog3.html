Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Abu Dhabi

Your journey begins with a gathering at Terminal 3 Ultimate at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport for your flight to Abu Dhabi

  • Day 02: Abu Dhabi – Paris (France) (Breakfast)

Arrive in Paris, the city of romance and lights, and begin your day by visiting some of its most iconic landmarks. Start with the Arc de Triomphe, a monument that honors those who fought and died in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars. Then, take a stroll along the Champs-Élysées, a vibrant avenue filled with shops and cafes, before heading to the Eiffel Tower for a photo stop at this world-famous symbol of France. Next, visit Les Invalides, a historic complex housing museums and Napoleon Bonaparte’s tomb, followed by a visit to the Grand Mosque of Paris, where you can admire its exquisite architecture. Explore the Latin Quarter, known for its narrow streets, vibrant atmosphere, and local shops perfect for souvenir hunting. End the day with a visit to Galeries Lafayette, a luxury department store renowned for its impressive dome and an extensive selection of high-end brands.

  • Day 03: Paris – Brussels (Belgium) – Amsterdam (Netherlands) (Breakfast)

Depart for Brussels, the capital of Belgium, and begin your exploration with some of its top highlights. Visit Manneken Pis, a quirky statue of a little boy that has become a symbol of Brussels. Then, head to the Grand Place, a UNESCO World Heritage site surrounded by ornate guildhalls, offering a glimpse into the city’s rich history. Explore the Grote Markt, the vibrant central square of Brussels, known for its stunning architecture and lively atmosphere. Afterward, continue your journey to Amsterdam, Netherlands, where new adventures await.

  • Day 4: Amsterdam – Zaanse Schans – Volendam – Koln (Germany) (Breakfast)

Explore Dutch culture with visits to Zaanse Schans, a historic windmill village that showcases traditional Dutch architecture and heritage, offering a glimpse into the country’s past. Next, head to Volendam, a charming fishing village famous for its picturesque houses and delicious seafood. After immersing yourself in Dutch culture, proceed to Cologne (Köln), Germany, to continue your journey.

  • Day 5: Koln – Cochem – Luxembourg – Nancy/Metz (France) (Breakfast)

Visit Cochem Castle (photo stop), one of the most beautiful castles in Germany, perched above the Moselle River offering stunning views. Continue to Luxembourg where you’ll explore the city’s highlights, including the Lady of Luxembourg Monument and the Old Town, which beautifully blends medieval charm with modern touches. Finally, travel onward to Nancy or Metz for an overnight stay.

  • Day 06: Nancy/Metz – Grindelwald – Lauterbrunnen – Zurich (Switzerland) (Breakfast)

Enjoy the stunning Swiss Alps with stops at Grindelwald, where you can admire the breathtaking Staubach Waterfall, and Lauterbrunnen, a picturesque village surrounded by majestic cliffs and serene beauty. End the day in Zurich, Switzerland’s largest city.

  • Day 07: Zurich – Feldkirch (Austria) – Vaduz (Liechtenstein) – Milan (italy) (Breakfast)

Travel through three countries with stops at Feldkirch Old Town, a medieval Austrian town with cobblestone streets and charming architecture, and Vaduz, where you can visit the town hall and the Liechtenstein Center, representing this small yet beautiful country. Continue the journey to Milan, Italy.

  • Day 08: Milan – San Siro – Serravalle Outlet – Milan Meals: Breakfast

Explore the fashion capital of Italy, Milan, where you’ll visit iconic landmarks such as the Galleria Vittorio Emmanuelle II, one of the world’s oldest shopping galleries, and the magnificent Milan Duomo Cathedral, a masterpiece of Gothic architecture. Enjoy a photo stop at the legendary San Siro Stadium, home to AC Milan and Inter Milan. The day concludes with a shopping spree at Serravalle Designer Outlet, featuring over 230 luxury brands at discounted prices, perfect for fashion enthusiasts.

  • Day 09: Milan – Abu Dhabi (Breakfast)

Transfer to the airport for your flight to Abu Dhabi.

  • Day 10: Abu Dhabi – Jakarta

Arrive back in Jakarta. Thank you for your participation, and we look forward to seeing you in our future tour programs.
