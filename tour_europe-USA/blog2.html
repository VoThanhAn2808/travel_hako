Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Hong Kong

Gather at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport to begin your European adventure, with your first destination being Paris, France.

  • Day 02: Hong Kong – Paris (Lunch)

Upon arrival at Paris Airport, embark on an exciting city tour showcasing some of the most iconic landmarks of Paris. Stroll along the famous Champs-Élysées Avenue and visit Place de la Concorde Square. Admire the historic Les Invalides, the stunning Petite Palais and Grand Palais, and the elegant Pont Alexandre III. Explore the architectural beauty of St. Magdalena and take memorable photos at the Arc de Triomphe and the Eiffel Tower. Conclude your day with a delightful Seine River Cruise, offering breathtaking views of Paris from a unique perspective.

  • Day 03: Paris – Brussels – Amsterdam (Breakfast, Dinner)

Travel to Brussels, Belgium, to explore the charming city highlights, including the iconic Manneken Pis statue and the historic Old Town. Afterward, continue your journey to Amsterdam, where you will enjoy an overnight stay.

  • Day 04: Amsterdam – Zaanse Schans – Volendam – Amsterdam (Breakfast)

Explore Zaanse Schans, a picturesque area known for its iconic windmills, cheese-making demonstrations, and traditional wooden shoe crafting. Next, visit the charming fishing village of Volendam, where you can capture memorable photos in traditional Dutch attire. Conclude the day with a relaxing canal tour in Amsterdam, offering unique views of the city’s historic waterways and architecture.

  • Day 05: Amsterdam – Cologne – Frankfurt (Breakfast)

Visit Cologne to marvel at the magnificent Cologne Cathedral, a stunning example of Gothic architecture and once the tallest building in the world (1880). After exploring this historic landmark, proceed to Frankfurt for an overnight stay.

  • Day 06: Frankfurt – Titisee – Schaffhausen – Zurich (Breakfast)

Journey to Titisee, nestled in Germany’s Black Forest, renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty. Continue to Rhine Falls in Schaffhausen, Europe’s largest and most spectacular waterfall. Conclude the day with an overnight stay in Zurich, Switzerland.

  • Day 07: Zurich – Mt. Titlis – Lucerne – Zurich (Breakfast)

Begin the day with a thrilling cable car ride to the snowy peaks of Mt. Titlis, standing at 3,020 meters above sea level. Later, head to Lucerne to explore its iconic sights, including the Lion Monument, Old Town, and Chapel Bridge. Take time to shop for renowned Swiss watches before returning to Zurich for the night.

  • Day 08: Zurich – Iseltwald – Interlaken – Zurich (Breakfast)

Discover the charm of Iseltwald, a quaint village by Lake Brienz, famous as a filming location for *Crash Landing on You.* Continue to Interlaken, renowned for its breathtaking Alpine scenery and serene atmosphere.

  • Day 09: Zurich – Vaduz – Innsbruck (Breakfast)

Travel to Vaduz, the capital of Liechtenstein, where you can visit the Vaduz Cathedral and the Kunstmuseum. Continue to Innsbruck, Austria, for an overnight stay.

  • Day 10: Innsbruck – Lago Braies – Lago Misurina – Bolzano Area (Breakfast, Dinner)

Head to Italy’s Dolomites region and visit the stunning Lago Braies, a beautiful alpine lake surrounded by dramatic mountain scenery. Continue to Tre Cime Lavaredo, one of the most iconic landmarks of the Dolomites. Then, explore the picturesque Lago Misurina and the charming town of Cortina d’Ampezzo, known for its pedestrian streets and boutique shops.

  • Day 11: Bolzano Area – Mt. Seceda – Malpensa (Breakfast)

Visit Mt. Seceda, one of the Dolomites' most stunning peaks, standing at 2,519 meters. After taking in the breathtaking views, continue your journey to Malpensa for an overnight stay.

  • Day 12: Malpensa – Hong Kong (Breakfast)

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight back home.

  • Day 13: Hong Kong – Jakarta

Arrive in Jakarta, bringing home beautiful memories. Thank you for your participation, and we look forward to seeing you in our future tour programs.
