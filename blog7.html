Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: HANOI ARRIVAL-CITY TOUR-SIC TOUR

Arriving at Noi Bai International Airport, you'll be welcomed and transferred to the city to drop off your luggage at the hotel before starting your Hanoi city tour. Visit the Ho Chi Minh Complex, including Ho Chi Minh's embalmed body and the One Pillar Pagoda. Explore the Vietnam Ethnology Museum (or the Vietnamese Women’s Museum if it's a Monday), enjoy lunch, and then visit Tran Quoc Pagoda, the Temple of Literature, and Hoa Lo Prison, a war history museum. Return to the hotel to conclude your tour.

Breakfast and lunch are included
  • Day 02 : HANOI-HOALU-TAMCOC-HANOI-SIC TOUR

After breakfast, you’ll be taken to Ninh Binh to explore Hoa Lu, the ancient capital of Vietnam during the Dinh and Le dynasties. Enjoy scenic rice fields and limestone outcrops before a 90-minute sampan cruise through Tam Coc, a UNESCO site. Visit the ruins of Hoa Lu and, after lunch, take a traditional sampan ride through rice paddies and the famous grottoes: Hang Ca, Hang Giua, and Hang Cuoi. Return to Hanoi in the late afternoon for an overnight stay.

Breakfast and Lunch are included
  • Day 03 : HANOI DEPARTURE

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast, enjoy some free time before we take you to the airport for your return flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
