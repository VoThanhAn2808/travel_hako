Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Incheon (Meals on Board)

We will gather at Soekarno-Hatta Airport Terminal 3 Ultimate for our flight to Incheon with Garuda Indonesia. Overnight on the plane.

  • Day 02: Incheon – Nami Island – Mt. Sorak (Meals on Board, Lunch, Dinner)

Upon arrival at Incheon, you will be welcomed by a friendly Indonesian-speaking guide. Our first stop is Songdo Central Park, offering a panoramic view of the new urban landscape in Incheon. Next, we will visit Nami Island, famous for being a filming location of the popular Korean drama Winter Sonata. Afterward, we will head to Mt. Sorak and check into the Sorak For You Resort. Relax and enjoy the evening. Overnight in Mt. Sorak.

  • Day 03: Mt. Sorak – Suwon (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

After breakfast, we will visit Gwangumsung Fortress Hill (with cable car) and the Great Bronze Buddha Statue at Sinheungsa Temple. Then, we will head to Suwon to visit the Mr. Toilet Museum and the UNESCO-listed Suwon Hwaseong Fortress. Check-in at JK Blossom Hotel. Overnight in Suwon.

  • Day 04: Suwon – Seoul City Tour (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast at the hotel, we will visit Everland, the largest theme park in Korea. Later, we will visit N-Seoul Tower, where you can leave a love lock, and explore Donuimun Museum Village. At hiKR Ground, you can take photos like a K-Pop star! Return to the hotel for check-in and rest. Overnight in Seoul at JK Blossom Hotel.

  • Day 05: Seoul City Tour (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast, we will visit the National Ginseng Museum, followed by a stop at a K-Cosmetic shop. Next, we will pass by the Blue House and visit Gyeongbokgung Palace and the National Folk Village. We will then explore Bukchon Hanok Village and take a walk along Cheonggyecheon Stream. Shopping at Dongdaemun follows before heading back to the hotel. Overnight in Seoul at JK Blossom Hotel.

  • Day 06: Seoul City Tour (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast, we will visit a Health Care Shop (Red Pine Tree) and Amethyst Showcase. We will then proceed to Itaewon Mosque, where Muslim guests can pray. Non-Muslim guests can explore the popular Noksapyeong Street from the drama Itaewon Class. K-pop fans will enjoy a visit to the COEX Mall, followed by a stop at Lotte Tower for photos. Lastly, we will visit Seokchon Lake Park, home to Lotte World. Overnight in Seoul at JK Blossom Hotel.

  • Day 07: Seoul City Tour (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast, we will explore Insadong Antique Street and Hongdae Street. You’ll also have the chance to make Kimchi and try on traditional Hanbok. Afterward, we’ll visit Duty-Free Shops and then shop in the Myeongdong shopping district. Check-in and rest at the hotel. Overnight in Seoul at JK Blossom Hotel.

  • Day 08: Seoul – Jakarta (Breakfast, Meals on Board)

After breakfast, we will visit a local supermarket for last-minute souvenirs before heading to Incheon Airport for your flight back to Jakarta.
