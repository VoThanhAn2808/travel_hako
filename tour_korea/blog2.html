Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Jakarta- Incheon

Today, we will depart from Soekarno-Hatta International Airport to Korea.

  • Day 2: Incheon- Nami Island- MT. Sorak (Lunch and dinner)

Upon arrival in Seoul, you will be taken to enjoy the scenic beauty of Nami Island, the filming location of “Winter Sonata”. You will also visit Soyanggang Sky Walk, the longest transparent sky bridge in South Korea. Afterward, you will be transferred to Mt. Sorak for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3: MT. Sorak – Gyeonggy (Breakfast, lunch, and dinner)

After breakfast, you will visit Mt. Sorak National Park and take a cable car (weather permitting) to Gwomgeumsung Fortress, where you can also see the Grand Bronze Buddha at Shinheungsa Temple. The journey will then continue to Gyeonggi for an overnight stay.

  • Day 4: Gyeonggy- Seoul (Breakfast and lunch)

This morning, you’ll visit Gyeongbok Palace, the iconic filming location for the drama Kingdom (if it’s a Tuesday, Gyeongbok Palace will be replaced by Deoksu Palace), the National Folklore Museum, and take a drive-by of the Blue House, the official residence of South Korea’s president. In the afternoon, you’ll stroll through Ikseondong Hanok Café Street, a charming area with traditional Korean houses and cozy cafés.

  • Day 5: Seoul-(Breakfast and lunch)

This morning, you’ll learn the art of making Kimchi and try on traditional Korean Hanbok. You will also visit a Ginseng Outlet & Cosmetic Shop. Afterward, enjoy a peaceful visit to Cheonggyecheon Stream. Finally, we’ll take you to N’Seoul Tower for a memorable photo opportunity.

  • Day 6: Seoul-(Breakfast and lunch)

Today, you will have the opportunity to shop for famous Korean products at the Red Pine Shop. Your journey will then continue with a visit to Eunpyeong Hanok Village, where you can explore traditional Korean houses. In the afternoon, you’ll be taken to the Duty Free Shop and the Dongdaemun Fashion Market for shopping.

  • Day 7: Incheon-Jakarta

This morning, you will visit a Korean Food and Souvenir Supermarket to shop for snacks and typical Korean souvenirs. Afterward, you will be transferred to the airport for your return flight to your home country. Thank you for your participation, and we look forward to seeing you on another one of our tour programs.
