Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Singapore – Busan (Meals on Board)

Today, we gather at Soekarno-Hatta Airport Terminal 3 Ultimate for our flight to Busan with a stopover in Singapore. Upon arrival in Singapore, we will visit Jewel Singapore Changi Airport with the premium airline, Singapore Airlines. Overnight on the plane.

  • Day 02: Busan City Tour (Meals on Board, Lunch, Dinner)

Upon arrival at Gimhae International Airport, Busan, you will be greeted by a friendly Indonesian-speaking guide. We will immediately head to Gaya Theme Park, the first extreme theme park in Korea and one of the filming locations for Running Man. After that, we will visit Haeundae Beach, a beach near the city center, and Haedong Yonggungsa Temple, located by the sea. Next, we will visit Oryukdo Skywalk, a glass bridge that juts out into the sea in a U-shape. We will also enjoy the evening view of Busan while crossing Gwanganri Bridge. Check-in at the hotel. Overnight at Arban Hotel Busan/similar.

  • Day 03: Busan – Seoul (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Breakfast at the hotel. We will go shopping at Kukje Market, Biff Market, and Jagalchi Market, famous for the BIFF "Busan International Film Festival" Street. Then, we will visit Gamcheon Culture Village, known as the "Machu Picchu of Korea" because of its colorful and artistic buildings. Afterward, we will return to Seoul by bus. Check-in at the hotel. Overnight at Golden City Dongdaemun Hotel/similar.

  • Day 04: Seoul (Breakfast, Lunch)

Breakfast at the hotel. Today, we will visit Gangnam K-Star Road, where K-pop fans can take photos with famous K-pop bear statues. We will also shop at Gangnam Underground Shopping Center and Gangnam Garosugil. Then, we will head to Gangnam COEX Mall to visit the Starfield Library, the largest and most popular library in the center of the shopping mall. We will also stop at Lotte Tower, the tallest building in Korea (photo stop), and visit Seokchon Lake, the most famous lake in Korea. Check-in at the hotel. Overnight at Golden City Dongdaemun Hotel/similar.

  • Day 05: Seoul City Tour (Breakfast, Lunch)

Breakfast at the hotel. We will visit the National Ginseng Museum and K-Cosmetic Shop, the best cosmetics center in Korea. After that, we will pass by the Blue House and visit Gyeongbokgung Palace, where we will wear Hanbok and tour the palace. Next, we will visit Bukchon Hanok Village to see traditional Korean houses, Hanok. We will also pass through Cheonggyecheon Stream to reach Dongdaemun for shopping. Check-in at the hotel. Overnight at Golden City Dongdaemun Hotel/similar.

  • Day 06: Seoul City Tour (Breakfast, Lunch)

Breakfast at the hotel. We will visit the Health Care Shop (Red Pine Tree) and Amethyst Showcase. Today, we will visit N-Seoul Tower, where we can place a love lock on the Love & Lock fence. Then, we will head to Namsangol Hanok Village, where we can explore Hanok houses and their interiors. Afterward, we will visit Itaewon Mosque, where those who wish can pray, and others can visit Noksapyeong Street, famous for the drama Itaewon Class. Then, we will shop at Duty-Free and Myeongdong Street. Before heading to Incheon Airport, we will stop by a local supermarket for souvenirs. Finally, we will be transferred to the airport for the flight back.

  • Day 07: Singapore – Jakarta (Meals on Board)

We will continue our journey to Jakarta and look forward to welcoming you on our next tour.
