Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Chongqing

Today, you will gather at Soekarno-Hatta Airport to begin your journey to Chongqing, the largest metropolitan city in China. Upon arrival, you will be picked up and transferred directly to the hotel for rest.

  • Day 02: Chongqing- Qianjiang (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)

Today, you will experience something unique by riding the Liziba Light Rail Through the Building, famous for its train that passes through an apartment complex. This station has gained worldwide attention for its remarkable design since its establishment. Every visitor, whether riding the train or observing from below, is amazed by how the train appears to "disappear" into the apartment building. Afterward, you will visit the Great Hall of the People, a significant landmark symbolizing the determination of Chongqing's citizens to build a better future and representing their hard work in creating remarkable achievements.

  • Day 03: Qianjiang-Fenghuang (Breakfast and Lunch)

Today, you will visit Fenghuang, also known as Phoenix Town. The town is named after a legend where two phoenixes flew over and were so captivated by its beauty that they hovered and refused to leave. In the Fenghuang Ancient City, you will explore the Wanming Tower, Shawan Scenic Spot, Ancient Stone Slate Street, and Bar Street. Dinner will be at your own arrangement. In the evening, enjoy free time to admire the stunning beauty of Fenghuang while sampling various local delicacies available in the ancient town before returning to the hotel.

  • Day 04: Fenghuang –Yongshun- Zhangjiejia (Breakfast and Lunch)

You will begin the tour by visiting Furong Ancient Town, a historical site over 2,000 years old inhabited by the Tujia ethnic group. Located in Hunan Province between the Avatar Mountain and Fenghuang Ancient Phoenix Village, this picturesque town is uniquely perched atop a cliff with a waterfall flowing into the You River. In 1997, Furong was renamed Hibiscus Town after becoming the filming location for the award-winning movie Hibiscus Town, which won the Golden Award for Best Chinese Film. The journey continues with a visit to one of Chongqing's landmarks, the 72 Tujia Stilted Buildings, renowned for their construction using wood, bamboo, and other local materials. These buildings are designed to withstand the humid climate and heavy rainfall while reflecting the lifestyle, customs, and beliefs of the Tujia people.

  • Day 05: Zhangjiajie (Lunch, and Dinner)

Today, you will have free time to explore Zhangjiajie at your own pace or join an optional tour offered during the day. Later, you will visit a Herbal Medicine Shop and the Junsheng Art Academy, renowned for its unique sand paintings. Here, you can admire a variety of sand artworks on display and appreciate the distinctiveness of each piece.

  • Day 06: Zhangjiajie (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)

This morning, you will visit Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, home to the Bailong Elevator, the only lift in the world built into a mountain. The lift will take you to the peak of Tianzishan Mountain, also known as Avatar Mountain. You will also visit Golden Whip Brook, located in the eastern part of the Zhangjiajie National Forest Park. The river, which flows through Golden Whip Rock, is 7.5 kilometers long and offers breathtaking views. After that, you'll experience a Tea Ceremony at a Tea House. In the evening, you will be transferred to your hotel for rest.

  • Day 07: Zhangjiajie - Pengshui (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)

After breakfast, you will visit a Jewelry Shop and a Latex Shop. Your journey will continue to The Grand Canyon, located in Cili County, Zhangjiajie. Upon arrival, you will head to the famous Glass Bridge, which is one of the main attractions in Zhangjiajie. You will also experience a Cruise Boat ride, visit Rainbow Square, try out the VR Experience, and more. This area offers stunning natural scenery and fresh air, making it a perfect place to enjoy the beauty of nature.

  • Day 08: Pengshui-Chongqing (Breakfast and Lunch)

Today, you will be escorted back to Chongqing. Upon arrival, you will visit the beautiful Ciqikou Ancient Town, also known as Baiyanchang. During the Qing Dynasty, the town flourished due to the porcelain industry. After that, you will be taken to see the main attractions of Hongya Cave, formerly known as Hongya Gate, one of the gates in the old city of Chongqing. Here, you can admire traditional Bayu architecture called "Diaojiaolou". Next, you will visit the Kuixing Building, a remarkable structure that connects the 1st floor of a rectangular building to the 22nd floor of the building across from it. You will enter from the 1st floor, but when you look down, there are 22 floors below. Afterward, you will head to the Liberation Monument, listed as one of the "Ten New Landmarks of Chengdu-Chongqing Cultural Tourism." You will also have the chance to stroll along Liberation Monument Walking Street and Bayi Road Food Street. At the scheduled time, you will be escorted to the airport for your return flight to Jakarta.

  • Day 09: Chongqing- Jakarta

Upon arrival in Jakarta, thank you for your participation in this tour. We look forward to seeing you on our next tour event.
