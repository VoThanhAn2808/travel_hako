Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Hanoi Arrival - City Tour ( Dinner )

Upon arrival at Noi Bai Airport in Hanoi, our tour guide and driver will warmly welcome you and transfer you to Vietnam's charming capital. We’ll start a half-day city tour, beginning at the Ho Chi Minh Complex and Ba Dinh Square then to Ho Chi Minh’s stilt house, and the iconic One Pillar Pagoda. Next, we’ll explore the Temple of Literature, Vietnam’s first national university from the 11th century. Then, enjoy a walk through Hanoi’s Old Quarter and stop at a famous café to try Hanoi’s unique Egg Coffee. Afterward, check into your hotel and relax. In the evening, savor a welcome dinner featuring authentic Vietnamese dishes at a local restaurant. Stay in Ha Noi.

  • Day 2: Hanoi - Halong Bay - Overnight on cruise ( Breakfast / Lunch/Dinner )

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Halong Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning limestone islands. Upon arrival, board your cruise and check into your private cabin. Enjoy a delicious seafood lunch while sailing through the bay’s breathtaking scenery. In the afternoon, visit a beautiful cave and relax on a secluded beach for swimming. Later, return to the cruise to take in the sunset over the bay from the sundeck. Enjoy dinner onboard and spend the night on the cruise in Ha Long.

  • Day 3: Halong Bay - Hanoi - Flight to Danang ( Breakfast / Brunch )

Wake up early for Tai Chi on the sundeck or enjoy the sunrise over Halong Bay’s towering islands. After a light breakfast with tea or coffee, take a sampan or kayak to explore Luon Cave. Return to the cruise to check out of your cabin as you navigate back to Halong City, taking in the stunning rock formations from the sundeck. Enjoy brunch onboard before arriving at Halong harbor. Our driver will transfer you to the airport for a domestic flight to Danang (approximately one hour). Upon arrival in Danang, our tour guide and driver will meet you and transfer you to your hotel for check-in. Enjoy free time at your hotel in Da Nang.

  • Day 4: Danang – My Son Sanctuary – Hoian Ancient Town ( Breakfast / Lunch )

After breakfast at your hotel, our tour guide and driver will pick you up for one hour to My Son, home to the ancient Cham Kingdom ruins, often called the Angkor Wat of Vietnam. Spend time exploring this historic site and capturing photos while our guide shares fascinating insights about My Son. Next, drive to Hoian for lunch at a local restaurant, followed by a leisurely walk through the ancient town. Visit Chua Ong Pagoda, the Chinese Assembly Hall, the 200-year-old Tan Ky ancestral house, and the iconic Japanese Bridge. Immerse yourself in the tranquil charm of Hoian, which thrived as an international port from the 16th to 18th centuries, attracting traders from around the world. In the late afternoon, return to your hotel in Danang.

  • Day 5: Danang - Bana Hills - Danang ( Breakfast / Lunch )

After breakfast at the hotel, enjoy a private transfer to Ba Na Hills, located 30 km from Da Nang city. Take a round-trip cable car ride to the summit and walk across the famous Golden Bridge. Explore the French colonial village, the stunning Nine Flower Garden, and Le Jardin D'Amour flower garden. Savor an international buffet lunch at Ba Na Hills before spending time at Fantasy Park, featuring indoor games and other attractions. After a fun-filled day, take the cable car back down and visit the bustling Han Market for some shopping. Return to your hotel in Da Nang to relax and enjoy an overnight stay.

  • Day 6: Danang - Flight to Ho Chi Minh City - Sightseeing Tour ( Breakfast / Lunch )

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and drive to the airport for your domestic flight to Vietnam’s vibrant metropolis, Saigon (Ho Chi Minh City). Arriving in the late morning, you'll enjoy an orientation tour through the bustling streets on your way to the hotel. Have lunch at a downtown restaurant of your choice before embarking on a guided city tour in the afternoon. Visit notable sites such as the Reunification Palace, the War Remnants Museum, Notre Dame Cathedral, the City Post Office, the Opera House, City Hall, and Ben Thanh Market. Enjoy the rest of the day at your leisure to explore more of this dynamic city.

  • Day 7: Ho Chi Minh City - Mekong Delta (My Tho - Ben Tre) ( Breakfast / Lunch )

After breakfast at the hotel, embark on a day tour to the Mekong Delta, specifically My Tho and Ben Tre, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Enjoy the scenic rice fields during the journey and visit the Vinh Trang Pagoda. Take a boat ride along the river, where you can observe stilt houses, fruit plantations, and fishing villages. Enjoy lunch in an orchard garden on Tortoise Islet, followed by a hand-rowed sampan ride through the natural canals. Savor seasonal fruits and honey tea while listening to Southern Vietnamese folk music. Explore a family business that showcases rural life, taste coconut candy, and stroll through fruit plantations. After a delightful day, return to Ho Chi Minh City for leisure time.

  • Day 8: Ho Chi Minh City - Departure ( Breakfast)

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast, enjoy some free time before we take you to the airport for your return flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
