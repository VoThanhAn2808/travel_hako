Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Singapore – San Francisco

Today, you will gather at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport for your flight to San Francisco via Singapore. Upon arrival in San Francisco, you will embark on a city tour to visit iconic landmarks, including the Golden Gate Bridge, Fisherman’s Wharf, Palace of Fine Art, Twin Peaks, and pass through Castro Street.

  • Day 02: San Francisco – Yosemite – Fresno (Breakfast, Dinner)

After breakfast, you will continue your journey to Yosemite National Park, famous for its granite cliffs and waterfalls. Later, you will head to Fresno for an overnight stay.

  • Day 03: Fresno – Las Vegas (Breakfast)

In the morning, you will travel to Las Vegas, the world-renowned city of entertainment and nightlife. If time permits, you will visit the Las Vegas Premium Outlet for shopping. This outlet offers American-branded goods, including Armani, Fossil, Hugo Boss, Tommy Hilfiger, Adidas, Samsonite, and more.

  • Day 04: Las Vegas Free Program (Breakfast)

Today is free for you to enjoy Las Vegas at your leisure. Alternatively, you can join an optional tour to the Grand Canyon West Rim to marvel at its spectacular natural beauty. In the evening, you can join another optional tour, the Las Vegas Old Night Tour, to experience the city’s dazzling lights and vibrant nightlife.

  • Day 05: Las Vegas – Page (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Today, your journey continues to Bryce Canyon, a national park renowned for its colorful limestone pillars known as "Hoodoos," which form a natural amphitheater. Later, proceed to Page for an overnight stay.

  • Day 06: Page – Laughlin (Breakfast, Lunch)

This morning, travel to Laughlin and visit Antelope Canyon, known for its stunning scenery. You will also visit Horseshoe Bend to admire the breathtaking views of the canyon and the Colorado River from 1,300 meters above sea level.

  • Day 07: Laughlin – Buena Park (Breakfast)

After breakfast, you will visit Cabazon Desert Hills Premium Outlets in Palm Springs for shopping. Brands include Michael Kors, Kate Spade, Coach, Tory Burch, DKNY, and more. Then, continue to Los Angeles for an overnight stay.

  • Day 08: Buena Park – Los Angeles – Singapore (Breakfast)

On your last day in America, you will go on a city tour of Los Angeles. Stops include Beverly Hills for photos and Hollywood Boulevard to see Mann’s Chinese Theater and the Walk of Fame. Afterward, you will transfer to the airport for your flight back to Jakarta via Singapore.

  • Day 09: International Dateline

  • Day 10: Singapore – Jakarta

Arrive back in Jakarta. Thank you for your participation, and we look forward to seeing you in our future tour programs.
