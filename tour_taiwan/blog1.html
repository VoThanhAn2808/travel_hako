Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta- Taipei

Today, you will gather at Soekarno-Hatta Airport to begin your journey to the island of Formosa, Taiwan. Upon arrival in Taiwan, you will be transferred to your hotel for rest.

  • Day 02: Taipei-the Martyr's Shrine -Taipei 101-Taichung (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast, you will be invited to visit the Martyr's Shrine and the CKS Memorial Hall, which is a memorial dedicated to Chiang Kai-shek. Then, you'll visit Taipei 101, the tallest building in Taiwan. Next, you will head to Vigor Kobo, a well-known souvenir food center in Taiwan. The journey continues to Taichung, where you will explore the Fongjia Night Market. You will spend the night in Taichung.

  • Day 03: Taichung- Taipei (Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast, you will be invited to visit Sun Moon Lake, where you can enjoy the beautiful panorama of the lake by ferry. You will also visit the Tsao indigenous tribe settlement and the Wen Wu Temple, the only historical temple in the Sun Moon Lake area. Afterward, you'll visit the Linzhi Showroom (a mandatory visit). Then, you will continue your journey to Taipei. Upon arrival, you will be taken to explore Ximending, a popular shopping and entertainment district.

  • Day 04: Taipei Free Program(Breakfast)

Today, you will have free time in Taipei (No guide and no transportation provided). You can explore the city at your own pace, visit your favorite spots, or enjoy shopping and local attractions.

  • Day 05: Taipei- Jakarta (Breakfast)

This morning, you will be escorted to the airport for your flight back to your homeland. Thank you for your participation with us. We look forward to welcoming you on our future tours!
