Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: HO CHI MINH CITY ARRIVAL

Sai Gon Arrive. Arrive at Tan Son Nhat International Airport. Receive a warm welcome upon arrival. Transfer to the city and check in the hotel. Free at leisure. Overnight in Ho Chi Minh City.

  • Day 02 : SAI GON - CITY TOUR

After breakfast at the hotel, start a full-day city tour of Saigon. Begin with the Reunification Palace, the former residence of the South Vietnamese President until 1975. Then, explore French colonial landmarks including Notre Dame Cathedral and the Central Post Office. Continue to Chinatown to visit Thien Hau Pagoda, dedicated to the Goddess of the Sea, and walk through the vibrant Binh Tay Market. After lunch, visit the War Remnants Museum, which displays artifacts from the Indochina wars, including military vehicles and tiger cages. Conclude the tour at Ben Thanh Market, where a wide variety of goods are available. End of services. Overnight in Saigon.

Breakfast and Lunch are included

  • Day 04 : SAIGON-MYTHO-SAIGON

After breakfast, transfer to the Mekong Delta at 8:00, enjoying a 1.5-hour drive through rice fields to My Tho. Visit Vinh Trang Pagoda, the largest in the region, before taking a motorized boat on the Tien River to explore Tortoise, Dragon, Phoenix, and Unicorn Islets. Enjoy tropical fruits and Southern Vietnamese folk music in a local village, then have lunch at a restaurant. In the afternoon, head to Ben Tre Province, known for its coconut products. Visit a coconut candy-making shop and take a rowing boat trip along palm-lined canals. Relax on a hammock or bike around the village before returning to Saigon. Overnight in Saigon.
