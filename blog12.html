Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Danang - Arrival - Hue

Upon arriving in Danang, you will enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before transferring to Hue via the scenic Hai Van Pass. Take a moment to stop along the pass to admire the breathtaking views of Danang City and Lang Co Beach. After your journey, check in to your hotel and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant. Spend the night in Hue.

  • Day 2: Hue city tour (Breakfast / Lunch/Dinner)

Starting with breakfast at the hotel, travelers will join a scenic boat trip along the Perfume River, enjoying the picturesque landscape on both sides. The first stop is Thien Mu Pagoda, a symbol of Hue with the 21-meter-high Phuoc Duyen Tower, built during the reign of Emperor Thieu Tri in 1844. Next, the group will visit the Hue Citadel, home to many historical sites like the Ngo Mon Gate and the tombs of Emperors Minh Mang and Tu Duc. Following this, there will be a visit to Dong Ba Market, the largest market in central Vietnam, perfect for shopping. In the evening, travelers are free to explore Hue’s nightlife and admire the illuminated Truong Tien Bridge along the Huong River.

  • Day 3: Hue - Bana Hills – Hoian (Breakfast / Lunch/Dinner)

After breakfast, check out and head to Ba Na Hills. Take the world-record-breaking cable car to Ba Na Hills station. If available, check in to the hotel (check-in is at 2:00 PM). Start the tour with Vong Nguyet Hills, Linh Ung Pagoda, and the Old French Villas. Continue by cable car to Nui Chua Mountain, the highest peak in Ba Na, to explore Nginh Phong top, Le Nim Villas, and the Orchid Garden. Visit the Debay Ancient Wine Cellar, Le Jardin D’amour flower garden, and the Golden Bridge with stunning views of Da Nang. After lunch, enjoy Fantasy Park with exciting games like 4D and 5D films. Capture photos at the French village, then take the cable car down and transfer to Hoian for dinner and overnight.

  • Day 4: Hoian city tour (Breakfast / Lunch/Dinner)

After breakfast, transfer to My Son Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site, to explore the ancient Cham ruins. Admire the intricate architecture and learn about the Cham civilization’s rich history in this spiritual setting. Then, return to Hoian to visit Cam Thanh Village and enjoy a bamboo basket boat ride through the coconut jungle and surrounding farms. Back in Hoian, have lunch before a city tour through the ancient town, visiting the Japanese Covered Bridge, Chinese Assembly Halls, and traditional 18th-century Vietnamese houses. Enjoy some free time for shopping or relaxing, then explore the town and its street food in the evening. Overnight in Hoian.

  • Day 5: Hoian - Danang city tour (Breakfast / Lunch/Dinner)

After breakfast, transfer to Da Nang for a city tour, beginning with the stunning Marble Mountains. Take time to explore its intriguing caves, ancient pagodas, and panoramic views from the peaks. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant, savoring Vietnamese cuisine. In the afternoon, visit Son Tra Linh Ung Pagoda, famously known for the towering Lady Buddha statue. Set on the peaceful Son Tra Peninsula, this statue of the Goddess of Mercy looks out over the sea, symbolizing protection for local fishermen. Afterward, check in to your hotel in Da Nang, where you’ll have some leisure time to relax or explore. End the day with dinner at a local restaurant, followed by an overnight stay in Da Nang.

  • Day 6: Danang- Departure ( Breakfast)

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast, enjoy some free time before we take you to the airport for your return flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
