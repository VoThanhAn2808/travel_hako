Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Phu Quoc Arrival ( Lunch/ Dinner )

Our guide and driver will warmly welcome you at Phu Quoc Island Airport, then head to a local restaurant for a delicious lunch. Afterward, visit Dinh Cau Shrine to admire its beautiful blend of Vietnamese and Chinese architecture. Next, head to Sunset Sanato Beach Club, one of the island's most breathtaking spots, where you can enjoy the stunning sunset and take memorable photos. After the sunset, we'll transfer you to your hotel for check-in and rest. At 7:00 PM, enjoy a scrumptious dinner at a local restaurant, followed by a visit to the lively Phu Quoc Night Market. Overnight in Phu Quoc.

  • Day 2: Phu Quoc East & South Island Tour – Grand World Phu Quoc (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

After breakfast at 8:00 AM, we will explore Phu Quoc’s top attractions, starting with the Pearl Farm, where you’ll learn about pearl cultivation, and the Prison Museum, offering insights into the island’s history. Next, visit a Fish Sauce Factory to discover Phu Quoc's famous fish sauce, followed by the peaceful Ho Quoc Pagoda. Relax at Sao Beach and explore the charming Ham Ninh Village. After a delicious lunch, rest at the hotel before heading to Grand World Phu Quoc for exciting entertainment. In the evening, enjoy a delightful dinner before returning to the hotel for the night.

  • Day 3: Vinpearl Safari – VinWonders Phu Quoc (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

After breakfast at 8:00 AM, you will visit Vinpearl Safari, where you will ride through the wildlife park in a bus, experiencing the excitement of being surrounded by roaming animals and passing through a vast, diverse jungle. You will also enjoy the Animal Show (10:00 - 10:30), showcasing talented birds and promoting environmental conservation, with the chance to feed the animals. At 12:00 PM, you will have lunch at a local restaurant. Afterward, you will visit VinWonders Phu Quoc to enjoy various games and the aquarium. At 6:00 PM, you will have dinner at a local restaurant. Overnight in Phu Quoc.

  • Day 4: Speed Boat to 4 island Tour – Cable car to Thom Island (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner)

After breakfast at 8:00 AM, we will travel to the southern pier of Phu Quoc Island and board a speedboat for our four-island tour. We will visit Mong Tay Island, Gam Ghi Island, and May Rut Island to swim, relax on the beaches, and explore the coral reefs. Before 12:00 PM, we will head to Hon Thom Island for a buffet lunch and enjoy the beach and water park. Finally, we will take the Hon Thom Cable Car back to the main island, enjoying stunning views. Dinner at a local restaurant will conclude the day before returning to the hotel.

  • Day 5: Phu Quoc Departure ( Breakfast )

Have breakfast at the hotel and check out before 12.00 PM. Free and easy at your leisure until transfer to the airport for your departing flight home sweet home. End of our services. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
