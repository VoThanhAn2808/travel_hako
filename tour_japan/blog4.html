Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: Jakarta – Osaka

Today, you are expected to gather at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport to begin your journey to Osaka, Japan. After check-in and departure, enjoy a comfortable flight to Japan's second-largest city. Upon arrival in Osaka, you will be transferred to your hotel to rest after the long journey.

  • Day 02: Osaka – Kyoto – Gifu (X)

Upon arrival in Japan, you will be warmly greeted by your guide. First, we will head to Shinsaibashi – Dotonburi, a famous shopping district known for its iconic Glico Man billboard. Then, we will visit Fushimi Inari, a Shinto shrine that is the central temple of over 40,000 Inari shrines dedicated to the deity Inari. Afterward, we will visit Kiyomizu Temple, an ancient Buddhist temple that has been standing since 798 and has survived 10 instances of war and natural disasters. Finally, we will travel to Gifu for hotel check-in and rest.

  • Day 03: Gifu – Shirakawago – Takayama – Matsumoto (Breakfast)

After breakfast, we will visit Shirakawago Village, where you can see traditional Japanese houses called Gassho-Zukuri. Next, we will head to Takayama Old Town, a rural area with well-preserved traditional Japanese buildings that are still in use today. Here, you’ll have the opportunity to shop for unique Japanese souvenirs. Afterward, we will continue our journey to Matsumoto. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel and relax.

  • Day 04: Matsumoto – Mt. Fuji (Breakfast, Dinner)

After breakfast, we will visit Mount Fuji, the tallest and most beautiful mountain in Japan. Next, we will proceed to Oshino Hakkai Village, known for its eight natural spring water sources and traditional Japanese village atmosphere. We will then head to Lake Kawaguchi, where you can relax and take photos with the stunning view of Mount Fuji. After checking into the hotel, you can enjoy wearing the traditional Yukata and experience a soothing Onsen (hot spring bath).

  • Day 05: Kawaguchi – Gotemba – Tokyo (Breakfast)

After breakfast, we will visit Gotemba Premium Outlets, the largest outlet mall in Japan, where you can shop for famous international brands. Next, we will head to Asakusa to explore Nakamise Street and visit Tokyo Skytree for panoramic views of the city. Afterward, we will visit Odaiba, where you can see the large Transformer Robot Monument.

  • Day 06: Tokyo Free Day (Breakfast)

After breakfast, today is a free day to explore Tokyo at your leisure.

  • Day 07: Kuala Lumpur – Jakarta (Meals on Board)

Today we will travel to Soekarno-Hatta International Airport in Jakarta. Thank you for joining us, and we look forward to welcoming you on our next exciting journey!
