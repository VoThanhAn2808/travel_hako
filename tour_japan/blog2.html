Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Jakarta - Transit in Kuala Lumpur - Arrival in Osaka

We will meet at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport 3 hours before departure to begin our journey to Osaka, one of the largest and most vibrant cities in the Land of the Rising Sun, with a transit in Kuala Lumpur.

  • Day 2: Osaka Universal Studios- (Dinner)

Upon arrival in Osaka, you will be taken to visit Universal Studios Japan, the world’s largest and most magnificent film studio theme park. Enjoy thrilling attractions such as Super Nintendo World, Despicable Me Minion Mayhem, The Wizarding World of Harry Potter, The Amazing Adventures of Spider-Man: The Ride, Hollywood Dream: The Ride, Water World Show, Terminator 2 (3D), Shrek’s Adventure/Sesame Street – Movie Magic (4D), E.T Adventure, Snoopy’s Playland, Jurassic Park, and many more!

  • Day 3: Osaka- Kyoto -Toyohashi- (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)

Today, you will continue your journey to Kyoto to visit Kiyomizu Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most famous temples in Kyoto, as well as Fushimi Inari Shrine, known for its rows of red Torii gates along the entrance path. Afterward, you will proceed to Toyohashi for an overnight stay.

  • Day 4: Toyohashi- Hamamatsu- MT. Fuji- Gotemba premium outlets- Kawaguchi -(Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.)

This morning, you will continue your journey to Hamamatsu aboard the Bullet Train (Shinkansen), one of the fastest trains in the world. The trip will then proceed to Mount Fuji, one of Japan's top attractions. Afterward, you will be taken to Gotemba Premium Outlets for shopping. Tonight, you will stay in the Kawaguchi area, where you can experience traditional Japanese sleepwear, Yukata, and relax in a typical Japanese hot spring, Onsen (if conditions permit).

  • Day 5: Kawaguchi- Tokyo – (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Tokyo. Upon arrival, you will start a city orientation tour, pass by the Imperial Palace, and visit Tokyo Tower Food Town. Next, you will explore the Asakusa Kannon Temple, an ancient Buddhist temple built in 628 and completed in 645, and stroll through Nakamise Street, where you can try various Japanese street foods. Finally, you'll visit Ginza, one of Tokyo’s most famous shopping districts.

  • Day 6: Tokyo DisneySea- Narita- Kuala Lumpur- (Breakfast and Dinner)

This morning, you will be transferred to visit Tokyo DisneySea, a water-themed fantasy park with exciting attractions such as Rapunzel’s Lantern Festival, 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea, Journey to the Center of the Earth, Mermaid Lagoon Theater, The Magic Lamp Theater, Aquatopia, Storm Rider, Indiana Jones Adventure: Temple of the Crystal Skull, and more. After dinner, you will be transferred to the airport for your return flight to Jakarta via Kuala Lumpur.

  • Day 7: Kuala Lumpur- Jakarta

Today, you will return to Jakarta. Thank you for being a part of this journey, and we look forward to welcoming you on our future tours.
