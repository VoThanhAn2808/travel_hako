Brief itinerary

  • Day 1: Jakarta- X/Haneda

We will meet at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport 3 hours before departure to begin our journey to Sapporo, a charming city on Hokkaido Island, with a stopover in Tokyo. Please gather at the International Airport to begin this exciting journey.

  • Day 2: Haneda- Chitose- Otaru Otaru Snow Story Festival.

Upon arrival, you will be taken directly to Otaru City to visit the Otaru Music Box, the Sakaimachi area, the Kitaichi Glass Factory, and the Otaru Canal, where you'll have the chance to enjoy the enchanting lights beautifully arranged for the Otaru Snow Story Festival.

  • Day 3: Otaru- Asahikawa- (Breakfast and lunch)

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Asahikawa City to visit the Otokoyama Sake Museum and Asahiyama Zoo, famous for its King Penguin Parade. In the afternoon, enjoy some leisurely shopping at the Aeon Mall Asahikawa Station.

  • Day 4: Asahikawa- Sapporo- (Breakfast and lunch)

Today, you will head to Sapporo. Upon arrival, you will embark on a city orientation tour, including a visit to the Clock Tower, photo opportunities with the Sapporo TV Tower, and exploration of Shiroi Koibito Park and Odori Park. In the afternoon, you’ll have free time to shop at the popular Tanukikoji Shopping Arcade, one of Sapporo’s top retail destinations.

  • Day 5: Sapporo- Noboribetsu- Mitsui Outlet Park- Sapporo- (Breakfast, lunch, and dinner)

This morning, you'll head to Noboribetsu to visit Date Jidaimura, a ninja-themed park. After that, you'll travel to Kitahiroshima for shopping at Mitsui Outlet Park. In the afternoon, you'll return to Sapporo to rest.

  • Day 6: Sapporo free program- (Breakfast)

Today is your free day to go shopping, visit family, explore Sapporo, or relax at the hotel.

  • Day 7: Sapporo- Departure - (Breakfast)

Enjoy a complimentary and relaxed breakfast at the hotel before being transferred to the airport for your departure flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!
