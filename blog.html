Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: DANANG ARRIVAL-HOI AN-SIC TOUR

Arrive at Hanoi airport and be greeted by our driver. Transfer to the hotel for luggage drop-off. Next, visit Non Nuoc Stone Carving Village and explore Marble Mountains with its mysterious caves, ancient Linh Ung and Tam Thai temples. After that, shop for local specialties and depart for Hoi An Ancient Town, where you can walk around, shop, and admire the colorful lanterns at night. Finally, return to Da Nang for an overnight stay.

Dinner is included
  • Day 02: DANANG-BANA HILLS-DANANG-SIC TOUR

After breakfast, depart for Ba Na Hills, where you will take a cable car to visit the famous "Golden Bridge" and explore Le Jardin, Debay Wine Cellar, and Linh Ung Pagoda with its 27-meter high Buddha statue. Enjoy the games and activities at Fantasy Park. After a buffet lunch, relax and visit the peak of Chua Mountain for stunning panoramic views of Da Nang City and Da Nang Bay. In the afternoon, take the cable car back and return to the hotel for an overnight stay in Da Nang.

Breakfast and Lunch are included
