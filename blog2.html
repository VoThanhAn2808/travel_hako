Brief itinerary

  • Day 01: HANOI ARRIVE -CITY TOUR

Arrive at Noi Bai International Airport. Receive a warm welcome upon arrival. Transfer to the city and drop off luggage at the hotel. Then, have breakfast at a restaurant. Visit the Temple of Literature, the first university in Vietnam, followed by a visit to Hoan Kiem Lake (Restored Sword Lake) and Ngoc Son Temple. Take an electric-car tour to explore the Hanoi Old Quarter. At 14:00, check in to the hotel. Enjoy free time to explore the city. Overnight in Hanoi.

Breakfast is included
  • Day 02: HANOI-HOALU-HANOI-SIC TOUR

Breakfast at the hotel. Then, our guide and driver will take you to Ninh Binh. Visit Hoa Lu, the ancient capital of Vietnam during the Dinh and Le dynasties, surrounded by lush green rice fields and limestone outcrops. Explore the ruins before heading to Tam Coc, a UNESCO site with rainforests, valleys, and limestone cliffs. After lunch, enjoy a scenic sampan cruise through the three grottoes: Hang Ca, Hang Giua, and Hang Cuoi. Return to Hanoi in the late afternoon. Overnight in Hanoi.

Breakfast and Lunch are included
  • Day 03 : HANOI HALONG - NIGHT ON CRUISE – SHUTTLE BUS

After breakfast, check out and head to Tuan Chau Island. Once on the cruise, you'll get a welcome drink and hear about the trip plan. Enjoy lunch on board, then visit a pearl farm or another spot. Spend the afternoon cruising around Halong Bay or Lan Ha Bay. In the evening, the boat will stop for the night. Dinner will be served, and afterward, you can try squid fishing or enjoy fantastic movies in your cabin

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are included
  • Day 04 : HALONG – HANOI

Begin your morning with a refreshing Tai Chi session on the sundeck, followed by a light breakfast with freshly baked pastries and coffee. Continue your scenic cruise through the bay before enjoying a delicious brunch onboard. After checking out, the cruise returns to Tuan Chau Marina, where you'll disembark and head back to Hanoi for a relaxing overnight stay.

Breakfast/brunch are included
  • Day 05: HANOI DEPARTURE

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast, enjoy some free time before we take you to the airport for your return flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!

Breakfast is included
