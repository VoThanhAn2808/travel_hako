Brief itinerary

  • Day 01 : HCM ARRIVAL

Arrive at Tan Son Nhat International Airport and receive a warm welcome. Transfer to the city and check in to your hotel. Enjoy free time at your leisure. Overnight in Ho Chi Minh City.

Dinner is included
  • Day 02 : HCM - CITY TOUR

Start a full-day city tour of Ho Chi Minh City. Visit the Reunification Palace, Notre Dame Cathedral, the Central Post Office, Chinatown, and Thien Hau Pagoda. Explore Binh Tay Market and have lunch at a local restaurent. In the afternoon, visit the War Remnants Museum and end the tour at Ben Thanh Market. Overnight in Ho Chi Minh City.

Breakfast and Lunch are included
  • Day 03 : HCM-MY THO – BEN TRE – HCM

Travel to the Mekong Delta, passing through rice fields on the way to My Tho. Visit Vinh Trang Pagoda, the largest in the region, surrounded by fruit trees. Continue by motorboat along the Tien River to explore Tortoise, Dragon, Phoenix, and Unicorn Islets. Enjoy tropical fruits, traditional folk music, and visit a bee farm. After lunch, head to Ben Tre, famous for coconut candy. Take a peaceful rowing boat ride through coconut palm-covered canals, then relax or bike around the village. Return to Ho Chi Minh City in the afternoon. Overnight in Ho Chi Minh City

Breakfast and lunch are included
  • Day 04 : HCM – CU CHI TUNNEL- DEPARTURE

After breakfast, the tour guide and driver will pick you up from your hotel and head to Cu Chi Tunnels. Explore the Ben Duoc Tunnels, an important historical site from the war, with over 250km of tunnels. Watch a short documentary about the guerrilla war, then visit highlights such as the Water Well, ventilation systems, underground rooms, and the Hoang Cam Kitchen, which was used to hide the smoke. See the Engineer Workshop where weapons were made and stop by the souvenir shop. After lunch, return to Ho Chi Minh City and transfer to the airport for your departure flight. Thank you for choosing our tour! We’re glad to have served you and hope you had a fantastic experience. See you soon!

Breakfast and lunch are included
